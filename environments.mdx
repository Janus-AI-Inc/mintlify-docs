We deploy in days, not weeks. The Janus team works directly with your engineering team to integrate our eval infrastructure into your codebase and get you running thousands of tests quickly.

## What We Build

- **Forward-deployed eval setup**: We integrate directly with your codebase, configure judges calibrated to your KPIs, and get you running thousands of tests in days
- **Data source integrations**: Connect to your internal traces, logs, and APIs to generate realistic test scenarios that reflect actual tasks your agent faces
- **Synthetic task generation**: Domain-specific datasets built from your company data, covering chat, voice, and workflow agent types

## Getting Started

1. **Contact the Team**: Reach out to [team@withjanus.com](mailto:team@withjanus.com)
2. **Scope your first agent**: We'll identify the right agent and metrics to start with
3. **We set everything up**: Our team integrates the SDK, configures judges, and runs initial tests
4. **Start evaluating**: You'll have a working eval pipeline with results in days
